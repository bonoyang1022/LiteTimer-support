Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I set the timer?

To set the timer, simply swipe up or down to adjust the time and press start.

2. Can I change the timer's notification sound?

Currently, the timer uses a default notification sound. Customization options may be available in future updates.

3. What if the timer doesn't work when my phone is locked?

Ensure that notifications are enabled in the app settings. If issues persist, please contact us for support.
